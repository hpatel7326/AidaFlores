Acne FAQs

Before your consultation.

What makes Face Reality different from a regular acne facial?

A standard acne facial treats the visible breakout. Face Reality is a system — clinical-grade home care, in-studio treatments, and weekly accountability working together over 90 days. It addresses why acne is happening, not just what it looks like.

How long until I see results?

Most clients see meaningful clearing within 12 weeks. Many are fully clear by week 16. Some skins are faster, some need longer — we go at your skin's pace, not a script.

Will my skin get worse before it gets better?

Sometimes there's a "purge" in the first 2–4 weeks as deeper congestion surfaces. It's temporary, expected, and a sign the program is working. We prepare you for it at consultation so it's never a surprise.

What if I'm already on prescription medications?

Many clients come in already on or coming off prescriptions. We'll coordinate around what you're using and modify the protocol so we never over-treat the skin.

Do you treat teen acne?

Yes — and we welcome parents at the first visit. The protocol is the same; the education just adapts to fit a teen's life.

What does the program cost in total?

The 90-day program typically runs $850–$1,200 (consult + in-studio treatments + Face Reality product). Exact pricing is reviewed at your consultation so there are no surprises.

Where is the studio?

The studio is in Huntersville, North Carolina — convenient to Cornelius, Davidson, Mooresville, Concord, and greater Charlotte. Exact address is sent in your booking confirmation.
