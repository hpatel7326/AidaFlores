Common questions

Before you book.

If you don't see your question here, send a DM on Instagram or call the studio — Aida answers directly.

How does the Face Reality Acne Program work?

It starts with a 60-minute consultation where we map your skin's history, triggers, and current state. From there, I build a custom home-care routine and we meet every two weeks for in-studio treatments. Most clients see meaningful clearing within 90 days, with full results around week 16.

Do you take walk-ins?

No — every visit is booked in advance so you have my full attention. The fastest way to schedule is through Vagaro, but you can also call or DM and I'll get back to you within a business day.

Where is the studio located?

The studio is in Huntersville, North Carolina — convenient to Cornelius, Davidson, Mooresville, and greater Charlotte / Lake Norman. Exact address and parking details are sent in your booking confirmation.

Are chemical peels safe for my skin?

The peels I use are professional-grade and prescribed based on your skin's tolerance, not a one-size-fits-all menu. We always start gentle and progress only when your barrier is ready. If it isn't, we wait.

How often should I come in for a facial?

For corrective work, every 2 weeks. For maintenance facials and brows/lashes, every 4–6 weeks is the sweet spot. We'll figure out the right cadence at your first visit.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes — purchasable instantly through Vagaro. They're delivered by email and can be used for any service.
