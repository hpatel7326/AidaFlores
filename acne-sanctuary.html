— acne FAQs

Before your consultation.

Have a question that isn't here? DM on Instagram or call — Aida responds personally.

What makes Face Reality different from a regular acne facial?

A facial treats the visible breakout. Face Reality is a system — clinical-grade home care, in-studio treatments, and accountability working together over 90 days. It addresses why acne is happening, not just what it looks like.

How long until I see results?

Most clients see meaningful clearing within 12 weeks. Many are fully clear by week 16. Some skins are faster; some need longer. We go at your skin's pace.

Will my skin get worse before it gets better?

Sometimes there's a "purge" in the first 2–4 weeks as deeper congestion surfaces. It's temporary and expected — we prepare you for it at consultation so it's never a surprise.

I'm already on prescription medications — does that matter?

Many clients come in already on or coming off prescriptions. We coordinate around what you're using so we never over-treat the skin.

Do you treat teen acne?

Yes — and parents are welcome at the first visit. The protocol is the same; the education adapts to a teen's life.

What does the program cost?

Typically $850–$1,200 (consult + treatments + product) over 90 days. Exact pricing reviewed at consultation so there are no surprises.

Where is the studio?

Huntersville, NC — convenient to Cornelius, Davidson, Mooresville, and greater Charlotte. Exact address sent in your booking confirmation.
