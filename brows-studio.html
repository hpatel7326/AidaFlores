Common questions

Before you book.

How long does brow lamination last?

Most clients get 6–8 weeks of hold before the shape starts to soften. We pair lamination with a brow wax & tint every 3–4 weeks in between to keep the look polished.

Korean lash lift vs. classic lash lift — what's the difference?

Korean lifts use a gentler chemistry that lifts from the root for a softer, more "awake" angle. Classic lifts curl the lash itself. Both work; Korean tends to suit more lash types.

Can I wear makeup after?

Skip eye makeup and water for the first 24 hours after a lamination or lift. After that, normal routine is fine.

I have very thin or sparse brows. Is lamination right for me?

Lamination works best on clients with some brow density to shape. If your brows are very sparse, we'll talk through tint-only options or a slower approach at your first visit.

How do you decide on a brow shape?

I map the brow to your bone structure and natural growth pattern, not a trending shape. We'll talk through what you've tried and build from there.

I'm new to all of this. Where should I start?

Welcome — about half of new clients start here. A first-time brow wax + tint or a classic lash lift is a great introduction.
