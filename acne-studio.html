Common questions

Before you book.

What makes Face Reality different from a regular acne facial?

A facial treats the visible breakout. Face Reality is a system — clinical-grade home care, in-studio treatments, and accountability working together over 90 days. It addresses why acne is happening, not just what it looks like.

How long until I see results?

Most clients see meaningful clearing within 12 weeks. Many are fully clear by week 16.

Will my skin get worse before it gets better?

Sometimes there's a "purge" in the first 2–4 weeks as deeper congestion surfaces. It's temporary and expected — we prepare you for it at consultation.

What if I'm on prescription medications?

We coordinate around what you're using so we never over-treat the skin. Many clients come in already on or coming off prescriptions.

Do you treat teen acne?

Yes — parents are welcome at the first visit. The protocol is the same; the education adapts to a teen's life.

What does the program cost?

Typically $850–$1,200 over 90 days (consult + treatments + Face Reality product). Exact pricing reviewed at consultation.
