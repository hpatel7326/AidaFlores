The Face Reality Acne Program.

If your acne hasn't responded to the usual things — drugstore routines, antibiotics, hopeful purchases — there's a reason. It needs a plan, not a product.

The Face Reality protocol is a 90-day, esthetician-guided journey that combines clinical-grade product with bi-weekly in-studio visits and weekly accountability. Most clients see real clearing within 12 weeks.

— before you visit

Things you might be wondering.

If you have a question that isn't here, send a DM on Instagram or call the studio — Aida answers personally.

What happens at a first visit?

We talk. About your skin, your routine, the things that have and haven't worked. Then we look at your skin under a magnifier and build a plan together. You'll never be sold something you don't need.

How long is the Face Reality program?

A typical journey is 12–16 weeks. Some clients see clearing sooner; some need longer. Either way, we go at the pace your skin sets.

Where is the studio located?

Huntersville, North Carolina — convenient to Cornelius, Davidson, Mooresville, and greater Charlotte / Lake Norman. Exact address and parking are sent in your booking confirmation.

How often should I come in?

For acne work: every 2 weeks. For brows, lashes, and maintenance facials: every 4–6 weeks. We'll figure out your right cadence together.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We do not accept walk-ins. Every visit is by appointment so you have Aida's full, undivided attention — book on Vagaro, or call/text the studio to schedule.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes — instantly available through Vagaro and a lovely thing to give.
