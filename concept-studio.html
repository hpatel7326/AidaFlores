Common questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Where is the studio located?

The studio is in the Huntersville / Lake Norman area, serving clients from Huntersville, Cornelius, Davidson, Mooresville, Concord, and greater Charlotte. Exact address and parking instructions are sent in your booking confirmation.

How does the Face Reality Acne Program work?

It's a 90-day, esthetician-guided journey. We start with a 60-minute consultation, then meet every two weeks for in-studio treatments while you follow a custom home-care routine. Most clients see meaningful clearing within 12 weeks.

How long does a Korean lash lift last?

4–6 weeks for most clients. We'll set the right rebooking cadence at your first visit based on your natural lash cycle.

Do you take walk-ins?

No — every appointment is by booking only so you have my full attention. The fastest way is through Vagaro. You can also call or DM and I'll respond within a business day.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes — available instantly through Vagaro and emailed to the recipient. They can be used for any service.

Book your visit.

Most appointments are booked 1–2 weeks out. Acne consultations release 4 weeks at a time — first-come.
